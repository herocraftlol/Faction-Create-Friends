## ⚡ Why FactionPlugin?

Turn your vanilla survival server into an epic faction battleground! FactionPlugin gives your players everything they need to form teams, protect their base, trade with others, and climb the ranks — all in one lightweight, easy-to-use plugin.

> *"The only faction plugin you'll ever need for survival servers."*

## 🎮 Supercharge Your Survival World

### 🏰 **Team Up & Build Your Empire**

- **Create factions** with a single command: `/faction create <name>`
- **Invite unlimited members** and assign custom roles
- **Faction chat** — private communication with your team
- **Custom tags** displayed next to player names
- **MOTD system** — personalize your faction's message
- **Description** — tell the world what your faction is about

### 🗺️ **Claim & Protect Your Territory**

This is where survival gets serious. Your base, your rules.

- **Chunk-based claiming** — protect entire 16x16 areas
- **Griefing protection** — only faction members can break or place blocks
- **Individual player permissions** — choose who can build, open doors, or use buttons
- **GUI-based claim management** — no confusing commands
- **Claim visualizer** — see exactly what land is protected
- **Strategic territory control** — expand your influence across the map

### 💎 **Shared Faction Economy**

Build wealth together as a team!

- **Emerald bank** — deposit and withdraw from a shared vault
- **Transaction logs** — track who deposited what
- **GUI interface** — easy point-and-click banking
- **Team funding** — pool resources for expensive projects
- **Economic warfare** — raid enemy faction banks (future update)

### 🔄 **Secure Trading System**

No more scams, no more drama!

- **GUI-based trading** — visual, intuitive interface
- **Dual confirmation** — both parties must accept
- **Multi-item trades** — trade any combination of items
- **Emerald integration** — include emeralds in trades
- **Anti-scam protection** — items are held until both confirm
- **Cancel anytime** — abort trades before final confirmation

### ⚔️ **Power & Ranking System** *(Game Changer!)*

Your actions in-game determine your power level. Higher power = stronger faction!

#### 7 Legendary Ranks:

| Rank | Requirement | Passive Effects |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| 🪨 **Stone** | 0+ Power | — |
| 🔶 **Bronze** | 1,000 Power | Speed I |
| 🥈 **Silver** | 5,000 Power | Speed I, Haste I |
| 🥇 **Gold** | 15,000 Power | Speed I, Haste II, Jump I |
| 💎 **Diamond** | 50,000 Power | Speed II, Haste II, Jump I, Strength I |
| 💚 **Emerald** | 100,000 Power | Speed II, Haste II, Jump II, Strength I, Regen I |
| 👑 **Legendary** | 250,000 Power | Speed III, Haste III, Jump II, Strength II, Regen II |

> *Power is earned through kills, playtime, mob kills, blocks mined, and more!*

## 📋 Commands Quick Reference

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/f create <name>` | Create a new faction |
| `/f invite <player>` | Invite a player to your faction |
| `/f claim` | Claim the chunk you're standing in |
| `/f unclaim` | Unclaim your current chunk |
| `/f sethome` | Set your faction's home point |
| `/f home` | Teleport to faction home |
| `/f bank` | Open the faction bank |
| `/f trade <player>` | Initiate a trade with a player |
| `/f stats [player]` | View player statistics |
| `/f top` | View faction leaderboards |
| `/f classements` | View player leaderboards |
| `/f info [name]` | View faction information |
| `/f who` | See your faction's info |
| `/f leave` | Leave your current faction |
| `/f disband` | Disband your faction (leader only) |

## 🔧 Permissions

| Permission | Description | Default |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| `faction.use` | Access to all basic commands | All players |
| `faction.create` | Create new factions | All players |
| `faction.admin` | Admin commands, bypass claims | OP only |

## 🚀 Quick Start

```bash
1. Download FactionPlugin-3.2.4.jar
2. Place it in your server's /plugins folder
3. Restart your server
4. Type /faction and start playing!
```

**That's it.** No configuration required for basic use.
